Not a bank account. Community Credits is a software platform and ledger. Funds are held by each farm/co-op in its own FBO (“for-benefit-of members”) bank account.

1

Food-only Wallet

Members preload credits and redeem them for local food—never for cash. Clear statements and receipts keep trust high.

2

Strong Reserves

Default 80% reserves with nightly sweeps. Policy engine prevents dips; everything reconciles daily.

3

Working-Capital Option

Through a bank partner, farms may borrow up to 40% of their own reserves (business-purpose), while keeping at least 60% on hold.

How it works

A

Members Load

Card/ACH funds settle straight into the farm’s FBO Member Credits bank account. The wallet balance updates instantly.

B

They Buy Food

Credits auto-apply at checkout (market, online, or POS). Refunds and expirations follow simple, published policies.

C

You Reconcile

Nightly sweeps move the non-reserved portion to operating cash; dashboards show reserves and exceptions.
