How it works

1

Members Load

Card/ACH funds settle directly into the farm’s FBO Member Credits bank account. Wallet balance updates instantly.

2

They Buy Food

Credits auto-apply at checkout online or at market. Refunds/expirations follow simple, published policies.

3

You Reconcile

Nightly, the farm moves the non-reserved portion to operating cash; dashboards show reserves and exceptions.
